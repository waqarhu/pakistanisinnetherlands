Group Rules

Please adhere to these rules to maintain a respectful community

Discussion Topics

Avoid discussions on politics, religion, army and national security to prevent conflicts.

No Unauthorized Links

Sharing of social media, YouTube, TikTok links strictly prohibited unless approved by admins.

Language Etiquette

Avoid abusive or disrespectful language. Create a safe space for everyone to express opinions.

Direct Messages

Refrain from sending direct messages, especially to female members, to ensure privacy.

Charity Messages

Allowed only for global disasters with admin approval. Personal charity requests not permitted.

Specialized Groups

Use specific groups for Housing, License, 30% Ruling, Language, and Visa queries.

Important: Violations may result in removal without warning.
