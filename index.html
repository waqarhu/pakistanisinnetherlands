Arrival Checklist

Things to do after arriving in Netherlands

How to Contribute
  1. You will only have access to Add Comment which is at the top (right below the heading)
  2. In the Comments, add your information. PLEASE USE THE BELOW FORMAT:
    • (Your Name / Date / Small summary of the information you are adding for context)
    • (*insert information below)
  3. If your relevant topic is not in here, click on "Topics Suggestions to add" and add your suggestion for a relevant topic AS A COMMENT

Note: The relocation agents will guide most of it.

Initial Setup

  • Postpaid plan for mobile + cellular network (SIM card)
  • Temporary Travel Card (OV-Chip card)
  • Cash Euros
  • Groceries

Official Registrations

  • Apply for BSN and Residence Permit
  • Apply for Bank Account/Debit Card/other apps
  • Apply for health insurance (LoonZorg is the safest option)
  • Find General Physician (Everyone has their personal doctor)
  • TB and Skin Test
  • Register at municipality

Essential Apps to Install

  • Revolut - Safe online banking/crypto/budgeting/savings
  • Transferwise - Sending money to Pakistan
  • Omio - Travel to other cities/countries
  • 9292 - Travel info with actual prices
  • Flixbus - Travel to other cities/countries
  • Citymapper - Travel in the same city
  • Bla Bla car - Carpooling to other cities/countries
  • Google Translate - Download Dutch/French for offline usage
  • Buienalarm - Rain alerts
  • Meetup.com & Humans App - For socializing

Housing & Utilities

  • Apartment Hunting - Funda, Pararius, FB groups
  • Arrange Wifi
  • Utilities/taxes

Transportation

  • Order NS (yellow personal card)
  • Apply for Company NS travel card
