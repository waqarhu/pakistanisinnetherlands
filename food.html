Grocery Shopping

Supermarkets with Halal Options

Most groceries are available at major supermarkets:

  • Albert Heijn - Has dedicated halal meat section
  • Vomar - Local chain with good selection
  • Aldi - Budget-friendly option
  • Lidl - Budget-friendly with weekly specials
Desi Groceries & Specialty Stores
  • Google "Pakistani/Indian supermarket nearby" to find local stores
  • Available items: Masalas, spices, lentils
  • Frozen parathas and rotis
  • Pre-cooked canned meals (daal, curry, etc.)
  • Basmati rice, pickles, and chutneys

Takeaway Options

  • Doner Kebab shops - Most are halal, offer affordable and quick meals
  • Turkish restaurants - Generally halal, check individual locations
  • Shawarma places - Common in most neighborhoods
